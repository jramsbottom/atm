50 Years of Service to Our Community

For fifty years we have provided a free monthly magazine and newspaper to listeners in the greater Bath, Bristol and Weston area. The recordings contain no advertisements and are delivered free to the listener's door. We can even help if they need something on which to play them.

"I find it wonderful to think that the dedicated volunteers behind our Talking Magazine have now been producing it for 50 years!

This monthly magazine focuses upon the interests of seniors, offering a community and a voice but you are the link to put us in touch.

If you know a senior citizen who is visually impaired and who would enjoy our free magazine please ask their permission and let us know. We'll do the rest!"
